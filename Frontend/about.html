Project 3 :- Dogs vs Cats Classifier — PyTorch

About this project

This is my third project in the domain of AI/ML/DL whose AI model is made by using the Pytorch and concepts of CNN(Convulational Neural Network) and trained on Dog vs Cat classification dataset on Kaggle.

This Image Classification project detects dog and cat in your given photo.

This trained AI model detect the labels in image and gives you a Number(label) that it could find in your given image and also give a Confidence score that represents that how much confident model really is to give you that output.

Labels :-

  • cat
  • dog

So if you upload a image that belongs to any of these label's category then you will get a predicted class from model in this scenario dog and cat label and confidence score

Conclusion

I trained this model on google colab notebook's free GPU T4(it comes with a specfic duration of usage) for 15 epochs and then loss was about 1.1 something and i got 73% accuracy on testing dataset of Dog vs Cat classification dataset on Kaggle

This accuracy seems low but when i tested the model on the randomly images taken by google related to labels(Dog and cat) then i notice that model performing very well even for different images of same category.

So final conclusion is that this model can predict the images that are belongs to the category of labels of Dog vs Cat classification dataset images and can predict on also other images due to it is trained on a huge no. of sample images compare to my second project.

Fun Fact :- This time even when you upload your photo this model will definetly tell you label of dog and cat,may be it is finding the dog's or cat's facial features on your face.
